About Us

At the heart of maritime innovation, our dockyard is where engineering excellence meets oceanic grit. Specializing in vessel transfer from sea to land—both partial and complete—we provide comprehensive services that keep ships strong, seaworthy, and ready for the demands of the deep. From precision shaft alignments and robust engine overhauls to body plate replacements and full-scale vessel construction, our team blends time-honored craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. Every project we undertake is a commitment to durability, safety, and performance. Whether restoring life to a seasoned vessel or building a new one from the keel up, we stand as a trusted partner for shipowners who demand nothing less than excellence.
